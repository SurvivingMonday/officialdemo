Why Play Honey Pot?

Honey Pot teaches users the dangers of using public-access computers that often are honeypots that lure unwary users when they input their usernames and passwords. The game simulates a scenario where a player is placed in the shoes of a hacker, and is given a behind the scenes view of how various softwares tools like a key-logger is used to gain illegal access to sensitive data from public computers
